2023 BALLON D'OR - KOPA TROPHY NOMINEES

The 2023 Ballon D'or recognizes the best footballers in the world in the 2022-2023 season. The award will be given based on the results of the 2022-23 season. 30 players were nominated for the award on 6 September 2023, and the ceremony will take place on 30 October 2023.


The following players were nominated for the Ballon D'or:


Alejandro Balde #20 (Barcelona)
Jude Bellingham #20 (Borussia Dortmund/Real Madrid)
Eduardo Camavinga #20 (Real Madrid)

Gavi #19 (Barcelona)
Rasmus Hojlund #20 (Atalanta/Manchester United)
Jamal Musiala #20 (Bayern Munich)

Pedri #20 (Barcelona)
Xavi Simons #20 (PSV Eindhoven/RB Leipzig)
Antonio Silva #19 (Benfica)

Elye Wahi #20 (Montpellier/Lens)
